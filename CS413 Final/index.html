Slime Defense

Instructions!

Only a mouse is required to play!
Defend your home base from the invading slimes that are attacking relentlessly!
If you have enough money, click on a tower button in the game interface and click to
set the tower down. You cannot build a tower on the enemy path, outside of the game screen,
or on other towers!

Tip: Space is valuable because you can't sell, place towers down efficiently.
